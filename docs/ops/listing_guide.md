# Margin Runner Listing Guide

This guide outlines best practices for listing products on our target platforms: Amazon FBA, eBay, and Facebook Marketplace.

## 1. Amazon FBA

### Pricing Strategy
- **Target the Buy Box**: Price at or within 1% of the current Buy Box price.
- **Speed over Max Profit**: Arbitrage depends on velocity. We prefer a fast 30% margin over a slow 50%.
- **Price Undercutting**: Use the 2% undercut rule for initial listing if we need to secure the Buy Box immediately.

### Listing Photos
- **Use Manufacturer Images**: For most arbitrage, we attach to existing listings. Ensure condition matches exactly.
- **Verification Photos**: For our own records, take photos of serial numbers and box seals before shipping to the warehouse.

### Keywords
- Generally handled by the existing Amazon catalog. If creating a new listing, focus on Brand, Model Number, and Key Function (e.g., "Noise Canceling").

### Shipping Approach
- **FBA Small Parcel Delivery (SPD)**: Consolidate multiple items into one shipment to minimize inbound shipping costs.
- **Labels**: Ensure every item has a clear FNSKU label covering the original barcode.

## 2. eBay

### Pricing Strategy
- **Buy It Now (BIN)**: Preferred for most items. Set a price based on "Sold" listings, not current "Active" listings.
- **Promoted Listings**: Use 2-5% promotion rate for items with high competition.

### Listing Photos
- **Full 12-Slot Usage**: Take clear, high-resolution photos. 
- **Include Faults**: Photograph any box dings or imperfections to prevent "item not as described" returns.
- **Serial Numbers**: Include a photo of the serial number to deter swap-returns.

### Keywords
- **Title Optimization**: Use all 80 characters. Include: Brand + Model + Color + Main Feature + "New" or "Sealed".
- **Avoid Fluff**: Don't use "L@@K" or "WOW". Use descriptive technical terms.

### Shipping Approach
- **Calculated Shipping**: Use for heavy items (Soundbars, TVs) to avoid losing margin on long-distance zones.
- **Same-Day Handling**: Aim for 1-day handling to boost search rankings.

---

## 3. Facebook Marketplace

### Pricing Strategy
- **Pad for Haggling**: List 5-10% higher than your target price, as most local buyers will offer less.
- **Cash is King**: Prefer cash for local meetups to eliminate all transaction fees.

### Listing Photos
- **Real-World Photos**: Buyers trust photos of the item in a home setting rather than stock photos.
- **Seal Proof**: Show the factory tape/seal clearly to prove it's brand new.

### Keywords
- **Local Search**: Use common local terms. Include the specific brand name early in the description.

### Shipping Approach
- **Local Pickup Only**: For bulky items (TVs), focus on local pickup to save on shipping risk and cost.
- **Public Meetups**: Use "Safe Trade Zones" at local police stations.

---

## 4. Universal Best Practice: The 5% Buffer
Always include a 5% safety buffer in your final profit check. This accounts for platform fee changes, returns, and unexpected shipping costs.
